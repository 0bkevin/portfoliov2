## Overview

Knowfi was built as a dashboard case study for translating financial data into something actionable. The design aims to make spending patterns, goals, and alerts easy to understand at a glance.

## What I focused on

- A hierarchy that surfaces the right metrics first.
- A calmer visual language for sensitive financial data.
- Pattern-based sections that make insights easier to scan.

## Outcome

The interface balances dense information with restraint, giving the product a more deliberate and professional feel than a generic banking app shell.
