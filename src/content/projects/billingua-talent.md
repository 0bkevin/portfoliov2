## Overview

Billingua Talent is a case study about turning a fragmented hiring flow into a calmer product experience. The goal was to make sourcing, screening, and shortlisting feel structured without making the interface heavy.

## What I focused on

- Clear candidate comparison views.
- A workflow that works across languages.
- Compact status signals so hiring teams can scan quickly.

## Outcome

The final direction leans into strong hierarchy, generous spacing, and a visual system that feels more like a focused operations tool than a generic recruiting dashboard.
