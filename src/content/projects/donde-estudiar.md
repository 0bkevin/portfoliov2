## Overview

This project explores how to make education discovery feel less overwhelming. The product organizes programs and institutions into a simple comparison flow that puts the most relevant details first.

## What I focused on

- Search patterns that reduce decision fatigue.
- Comparison cards that stay readable on mobile.
- Content hierarchy that supports quick scanning.

## Outcome

The result is an editorial-style product narrative that feels practical and trustworthy, with enough structure to support both browsing and more deliberate evaluation.
