## Overview

SEA is a study case around operational security tooling. The product concept focuses on alert triage, evidence collection, and a workflow that helps teams stay oriented under pressure.

## What I focused on

- Strong state visibility during incident handling.
- Compact information cards for fast decisions.
- A layout that feels structured without becoming rigid.

## Outcome

The concept pushes toward a systems-first aesthetic with a product tone that feels serious, organized, and built for real operational use.
