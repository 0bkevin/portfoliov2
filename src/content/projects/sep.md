## Overview

SEP explores how to design a multi-step enrollment flow without making the process feel bureaucratic. The case study emphasizes clarity, reassurance, and visible progress.

## What I focused on

- A guided form experience that feels lightweight.
- Progress states that reduce uncertainty.
- Layout decisions that keep complex steps approachable.

## Outcome

The final direction is intentionally polished and restrained, with just enough visual structure to make the process feel trustworthy and easy to complete.
